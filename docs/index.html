So long, farewell. After making great strides over the past few years, we have made the difficult decision to close our doors.

We are incredibly proud of what we built. We’ll miss using it ourselves.

Although we designed a business that customers absolutely love, it proved hard to scale into the profitability it needed to be sustainable.

While succeeding in one goal, we couldn’t make the other.

Thank you to everyone who has been a part of our journey.

Background

Love Property, initially established in 2017 under the name "Realli," began as a platform dedicated to advertising and selling new home developments. Though we enjoyed initial success, the unforeseen impact of COVID and a decline in new home launches prompted a shift in our focus in 2020.

Embracing change, we rebranded to Love Property, adopting a fresh mission to curate and showcase the finest properties for both casual browsers and serious buyers. Throughout our journey, from Realli to Love Property, leveraging the power of social media and high-quality media content was our core focus."
